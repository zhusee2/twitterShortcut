TwitterShortcut

Safari Extension for quick taking you to Twitter with @username or #hashtag search in the smart search field.

Description

TwitterShortcut is a Safari Extension for quick access to Twitter. It takes advantage to the newly added “Smart Search Field” in Safari 6 as a shortcut syntax receiver. TwitterShortcut will recognize supported syntax and redirect you before Safari send the string to a search engine.

TwitterShortcut requires Safari 6 and above.

Usage

After installed, just type @username or #hashtag in the smart search field (the address field,) and you'll lead to corresponding Twitter page.
